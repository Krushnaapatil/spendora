# FAQ

## How does Spendora calculate savings?

Spendora uses centralized pricing data, plan thresholds, and deterministic optimization logic to estimate potential savings opportunities.

AI is used only for executive summaries and presentation enrichment.

---

## Does Spendora require login?

No.

Users can generate audits anonymously. Authentication is only required for saving and managing historical audits.

---

## Which tools are supported?

Current support includes:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Cursor
- GitHub Copilot
- Windsurf

---

## Are the recommendations AI-generated?

The pricing and recommendation engine is deterministic.

AI is used only for:
- summaries
- readability
- executive-style reporting

This improves consistency and operational trust.

---

## Can teams share reports internally?

Yes.

Every audit generates:
- shareable URLs
- public reports
- social preview metadata

making reports easy to distribute internally.
