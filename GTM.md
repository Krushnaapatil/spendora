# GTM — Spendora

## Positioning

Spendora is a **free AI tooling audit** for engineering and operations teams. It turns subscription data into a shareable savings report with deterministic recommendations.

**One-liner:** Find wasted AI spend across Cursor, Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, and more—in under 60 seconds.

## Why This Approach

Most AI cost management platforms target:
- enterprise procurement
- large finance teams
- centralized IT governance

Spendora instead focuses on:
- lightweight onboarding
- fast audits
- operational clarity
- immediate visibility

The goal is to reduce friction and allow teams to discover optimization opportunities before introducing heavier workflows or sales processes.

## Target customer

| Segment | Pain | Why Spendora |
|--------|------|----------------|
| Startup eng leads (5–30 seats) | Overlapping AI tools, wrong plan tiers | Fast audit, no procurement cycle |
| Ops / finance at SMBs | No line-of-sight on AI SaaS | Shareable report for budget talks |
| Agencies | Per-seat tool sprawl | Shareable audit links for clients |

**Primary wedge:** Teams paying for 2+ AI dev or writing tools.

## Acquisition

1. **Product-led** — Landing → `/audit/new` → shareable `/audit/[id]`
2. **GitHub / dev communities** — Open repo, deterministic engine builds trust
3. **Content** — Posts grounded in `PRICING_DATA.md` rules
4. **High-savings CTA** — Lead capture for consultation above threshold

## Funnel

```
Visit → Audit → Report → Email gate → Sign up → /audits dashboard
```

**Metrics:** audits/week, lead capture rate, auth conversion, shares sent.

## Pricing hypothesis

| Tier | Offer |
|------|--------|
| Free audit | Deterministic report + AI summary |
| Consultation | Negotiation / consolidation (Credex) |
| Future SaaS | Monitoring, re-audit alerts |

## 90-day focus

1. Trust signals ($/seat reasoning, sources)
2. Better recommendations (less generic)
3. User interviews (`USER_INTERVIEWS.md`)
4. Measure share → signup → repeat audit

## Long-Term Expansion

If retention proves strong, Spendora could expand into:
- recurring audit monitoring
- workspace dashboards
- spend alerts
- procurement visibility
- AI tooling analytics
- optimization benchmarking

The long-term opportunity is not just audit generation, but continuous AI infrastructure visibility for growing teams.
